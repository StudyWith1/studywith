About us

Our mission

The StudyWith platform is designed to provide people with the opportunity to learn in a convenient format and at a convenient time. With StudyWith, you can gain or share knowledge, as well as chat with other users who are registered for the course. An additional motivation for studying on our platform is the opportunity to receive a certificate that you can use in your studies/work/resume or other. Therefore, our mission is to create convenient, interesting and effective conditions for learning and sharing knowledge.

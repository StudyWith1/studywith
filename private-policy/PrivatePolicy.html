StudyWith Privacy Policy

A. Introduction

1. The privacy of StudyWith educational platform users is of paramount importance to us, and we are committed to ensuring its protection. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. By agreeing to the use of cookies during your first visit to our platform, you authorize us to use them during each subsequent visit in accordance with this Policy.

B. Collection of Personal Data

We collect and process the following types of personal data:

  • Device information, including IP address, geographic location, browser type and version, and operating system
  • Platform usage data, including browsing history, learning progress, and interaction with educational materials
  • Registration information: email, first and last name
  • Profile information: profile photo, education, professional experience, learning interests
  • Performance data: test results, completed assignments, obtained certificates
  • Payment information for course purchases (processed through secure payment systems)
  • Communication data: correspondence with support, platform comments, course reviews

C. Use of Personal Data

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing access to educational materials and platform functionality
  • Personalizing learning experience and course recommendations
  • Evaluating learning progress and issuing certificates
  • Communication regarding learning process and technical support
  • Improving the quality of our educational services
  • Processing payments and providing access to purchased courses
  • Sending important updates about the platform and new features
  • Protecting the platform from fraud and abuse

D. Disclosure of Personal Information

StudyWith may disclose your personal data in the following cases:

  • To our employees and instructors to the extent necessary for providing educational services
  • To service providers who assist in platform operations (hosting, payment processing, technical support)
  • In cases required by Ukrainian legislation and international law
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of StudyWith and other users

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except as described in this Policy.

E. Protection of Personal Data

To protect your personal data, we implement the following measures:

  • Use of encryption for confidential data transmission
  • Storage of data on secure servers with regular backups
  • Limited access to personal data only for authorized personnel
  • Regular security system updates and threat monitoring
  • Regular security audits

F. Cookies and Tracking

StudyWith uses cookies and other tracking technologies to improve platform operation. We use:

  • Essential cookies for platform functionality
  • Analytical cookies for platform usage analysis
  • Functional cookies for experience personalization

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies; however, this may limit access to certain platform features.

G. Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your StudyWith account. When you do:

  • Your profile will be deactivated
  • Personal data will be removed from the active database
  • Learning history and progress will be anonymized
  • You will be removed from mailing lists

Important: Some information may be retained in database archive copies or as required by legal obligations (e.g., course payment data).

H. Your Rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Restrict the processing of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing

I. Changes to Privacy Policy

StudyWith reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Email notification
  • Platform announcement
  • Updating the "last modified" date in this Policy

Continued use of the platform after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated terms.
